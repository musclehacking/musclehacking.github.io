How To Stay Weak

By Jay — Posted April 2024

  • Only go to the gym when you feel 100%.
  • If it’s raining, stay home.
  • If it’s too hot, stay home.
  • If your <insert body part> is sore, just wait a couple of weeks for it to feel better.
  • If it’s a holiday, skip the gym and watch some Netflix instead.
  • If you only have time for a 30-minute session, just skip the gym entirely.
  • If your gym partner is late, stay in your car outside and watch some TikToks.
  • If there are too many people at the gym, leave.
  • If the equipment you want to use is taken, just skip it and cut your session short.
  • Spend more time checking your phone than actually working out.
  • If you forgot your headphones, leave.
  • Always choose the easiest exercises to avoid breaking a sweat.
  • If you don’t see immediate results, lose motivation for a couple of months.
  • Skip leg day.
  • “Oh well, I guess I just don’t have the genetics for it.”
